OUR STORY


Founded in 2023, Dias Shop brings over a decade of experience managing the complexities of monetization and content discovery in gaming and beyond. Dias Shop is trusted by over 300 publishers – including industry giants Activision Blizzard, Bigo, Electronic Arts, Riot Games, and Zynga – to grow revenue, profit margins, and customer engagement by connecting them to 10M+ paying customers around the world. Dias Shop offers channel, platform and payment solutions including Dias Shop, which offers direct payments integration on publishers’ websites, and Dias Shop, the preferred destination for in-game content purchases for millions of gamers worldwide, and xShop, which makes their content available on a range of e-commerce platforms.
Headquartered in Philippines and backed by Nash Capital, Sight Partners, CGIC, Pis Partners, and GGMO Global Payment Fund, Dias Shop has been named one of the fastest-growing companies in TAPAC by the Financial Timers, a Technology Pioneer by the Worldwide Economic Forum and the BestBuyer Payment Solutions Provider for the Gaming Industry (Global) by Global Branded Magazine.
